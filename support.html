What is "Third Party"? ‚ñº

"Third Party" is an app for managing agreements between people. You can create structured deals, assign responsible parties, attach files, and track execution progress.

How to create a new agreement? ‚ñº

1. Tap the "+" button on the main screen
2. Enter agreement title and description
3. Add agreement items
4. Assign responsible parties
5. Attach files if needed
6. Save the agreement

How to add new participants? ‚ñº

Go to "Contacts" section and tap "Add Participant". You can invite people by email or phone number. After accepting the invitation, they will appear in your contacts.

Is my data secure? ‚ñº

Yes! All your data is transmitted via secure HTTPS connection and stored encrypted on our servers. We don't share your data with third parties without your consent.

How to confirm item completion? ‚ñº

Open the agreement, find the required item and tap "Confirm Completion". The responsible person will receive a notification about the confirmation.
